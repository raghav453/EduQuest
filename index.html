frequently asked queries

Is there any way to preview a course?

Yes! For steps on how to preview a course, and review key information about it and click on preview button

How do I take a EduQuest course?

EduQuest courses are entirely on-demand. You can begin the course whenever you like, and there are no deadlines to complete it.EduQuest courses can be accessed from several different devices and platforms, including a desktop, laptop, and our mobile app.After you enroll in a course, you can access it by clicking on the course link you will receive in your confirmation email (provided you’re logged into your EduQuest account). You can also begin the course by logging in and navigating to your My learning page.

Do I have to start my EduQuest course at a certain time?

As noted above, there are no deadlines to begin or complete the course. Even after you complete the course you will continue to have access to it, provided that your account’s in good standing, and EduQuest continues to have a license to the course.

How can I get my course certificate?

Certificate will be given once course and attached assignments are completed. If you failed to complete the assignments, kindly complete them coz they will let us know how much you understood.
